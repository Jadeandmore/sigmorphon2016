Welcome

The first SIGMORPHON shared task is morphological paradigm completion. The goal is to fill out a morphological paradigm, a nominal or verbal inflection table, given some incomplete representation of the whole paradigm, e.g., the lemma or a partial (potentially noisy) set of slots of the paradigm. This area of computational morphology has received a flurry of attention recently in the NLP literature (Dreyer and Eisner (2009), Dreyer and Eisner (2011), Durett an DeNero (2013), Ahlberg et al. (2014), Nicolai and Kondrak (2015), inter alia).
